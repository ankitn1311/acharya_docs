# 📝 Initiate Leave

The **Initiate Leave** module empowers authorized users to apply for leaves on behalf of employees, streamlining the process for HR personnel and department heads managing multiple team members.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 👥 **Bulk Leave Initiation**  
  Effortlessly initiate leave requests for one or more employees at once.

- 🗂️ **Centralized Leave Submission**  
  Streamlines the leave application process, ensuring consistent data entry and easy tracking across teams.

- 🔄 **Unified Workflow**  
  Once a leave is initiated, it follows the same approval and tracking process as leaves applied by employees themselves.

---

> 📅 **Ideal for:** Managing planned leaves during institutional breaks, public holidays, or department-wide time-offs.
