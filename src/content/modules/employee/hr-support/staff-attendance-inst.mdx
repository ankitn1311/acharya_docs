# 🕒 Staff Attendance – INST & DEPT

The **Staff Attendance – INST & DEPT** modules provide segmented attendance data, allowing users to view records by **Institute** and **Department** based on their respective access levels.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🏫 **Institute-Level Attendance (INST)**  
  View attendance records for all employees within your **institute**. Ideal for institute-level administrators and HR personnel.

- 🏢 **Department-Level Attendance (DEPT)**  
  Track attendance for employees within your **own department**. Perfect for department heads and managers.

- 📊 **Filterable Grid View**  
  Attendance data is displayed in a structured grid format, with filters for:
  - 📅 **Month of Attendance**
  - 🏫 **School/Institute**
  - 🏢 **Department**
  - 👥 **Employee Type** (e.g., Staff, Consultant)

---

> These submenus offer a quick and accurate way to monitor employee attendance trends, support payroll processing, and ensure departmental accountability.
