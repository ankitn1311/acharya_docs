# 📒 Employee Log

The **Employee Log** section provides a detailed, centralized snapshot of an individual employee's profile and related records for easy reference and internal use.

---

## 👤 Profile Overview

This section displays all key details of the selected employee, including:

1. 🏷️ **Name**
2. ⏰ **Shift**
3. 🧑‍🏫 **Proctor Head**
4. 👤 **Reported To**
5. 📝 **Leave Approver 1**
6. 📝 **Leave Approver 2**
7. 🏬 **Store Indent Approver 1**
8. 🏬 **Store Indent Approver 2**
9. 🎓 **PhD Status**
10. 🧾 **Personal Details** (Gender, Date of Birth, Marital Status)
11. 🏦 **Bank Details** (Account Number, IFSC)
12. 📄 **Document Details** (Aadhaar No., PAN, UAN, PF No., DL No., etc.)

---

> This log ensures all essential employee information is easily accessible for HR and management purposes.
