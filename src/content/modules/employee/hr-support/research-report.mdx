# 📚 Research Report

The **Research Report** module offers a comprehensive overview of academic research and thesis submissions by employees.

---

## 🔄 Profile Integration
- When a user updates their research profile from **My Profile**, it automatically becomes visible in this section.

## ✨ Key Features

- 📑 **Thesis & Research Tracking**  
  View thesis details and research submissions from employees across the organization.

- 👤 **Employee Research Profile**  
  Each entry includes:
  - 🎓 **PhD Status** (e.g., Pursuing, Holder)
  - 🏷️ **Designation**
  - 🏢 **Department**
  - ⏳ **PhD Tenure**
  - 🏫 **University Attended**
  - 📝 **Thesis Title**
  - 📅 **Registration Date**
  - ✅ **Completed Date**

---

> This module empowers HR, academic heads, and research committees to monitor research progress, maintain institutional records, and support employees in their academic pursuits.
