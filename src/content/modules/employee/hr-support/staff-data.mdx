# 👥 Staff Data

The **Staff Data** module provides a structured and organized view of all employees in the organization, categorized by employment type for easy reference and effective management.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. 🗂️ Distinct Employee Categories

The data is divided into two clearly defined sections:

- 👔 **Permanent Employees**  
  View details of all full-time staff members on the organization's regular payroll.

- 📝 **Consultants / Contractual Employees**  
  Access information for individuals working on a contract or consultancy basis.

### 2. 📋 Comprehensive Employee Information

Each section displays essential staff details, including:

- 🆔 **Employee Code**
- 🏷️ **Name**
- 🔄 **Onboard Type** (e.g., Full Time, Part Time)
- 🏫 **School**
- 🏢 **Department**
- 💼 **Designation**
- 👔 **Job Type**
- 📅 **Date of Joining**
- 📝 **Leave Approvers**
- ...and more

---

> This module enables HR and administrative teams to efficiently manage workforce information by clearly separating long-term and temporary staff records.
