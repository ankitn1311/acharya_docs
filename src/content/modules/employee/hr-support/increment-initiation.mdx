# 📈 Increment Initiation

The **Increment Initiation** module is designed to streamline the management and processing of employee salary revisions. It empowers HR and authorized personnel to efficiently initiate, review, and finalize increments.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🚀 **Initiate Increments**  
  Start the salary increment process for selected employees with ease.

- 💰 **Employee Salary Details**  
  View all relevant salary information for each employee, including:
  - 🏷️ **Name**
  - 🏢 **Department**
  - 💵 **Current CTC**
  - 📈 **Proposed/Updated CTC** etc.

- ✅ **Finalize Increments**  
  After reviewing proposed changes, finalize increments to send approval to the authorized person.

---

> This module ensures a transparent, well-documented process for handling employee compensation updates, fully aligned with internal approval workflows and organizational policies.
